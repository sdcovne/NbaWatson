Player Value (PV) and Potential Player Value (PPV)

Player Value (PV) is a metric that estimates the economic value of a single player considering his contribution in team's wins. Potential Player Value (PPV) instead is a metric that estimates the economic value of a single player considering his potential contribution in team's wins. Calculating PV and PPV is quite simple.
